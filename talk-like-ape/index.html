Game Rules

  • 1. NO SAY BIG WORDS WHEN PLAY Only speak in words of one syllable. If you say a big word, you get a foul and lose 1 point!
  • 2. NO SAY WORD ON CARD Describe the target word without saying any part of it.
  • 3. TEAM GUESS. NO PEEK AT CARD Only the describer looks at the card. The rest of the team must guess.

  • 🍌 Pure Caveman Style Charming, hand-drawn wobbly aesthetics and animations that wiggle and bounce with life.

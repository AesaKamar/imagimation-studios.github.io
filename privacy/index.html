Effective Date: March 19, 2026

ImagiMation Studios ("we", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This policy specifically covers Cat Game: Tadpole Chase and Bolo Baccho.

The App itself does not collect, store, or share any personal information such as names, email addresses, or precise location data. No account registration is required.

To keep the app free, Cat Game: Tadpole Chase uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Cat Game is designed specifically for pets and their owners, providing a safe and engaging environment. All ads are filtered to be family-appropriate and COPPA compliant.

4. Anonymous Usage Information

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data (such as interaction frequency) solely to improve the content and user experience. This information is non-identifiable and never shared with external parties outside of necessary crash reporting and analytics.

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple or Google. We do not have access to your credit card or payment information.

6. Compliance with COPPA

The App complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have mistakenly collected such information, please contact us immediately.
